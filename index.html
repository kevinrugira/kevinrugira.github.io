About Kevin Rugira

Hello, I'm Kevin Rugira, a 22-year-old with a passion for the intersection of Life Sciences and data science. I hold a Bachelor of Science degree in Biochemistry from the University of Nebraska-Lincoln, and my mission is to leverage this unique blend of disciplines to enhance people's daily lives.

When I'm not immersed in the world of data and molecules, you can find me exploring other passions. I find solace in coding, where I bring my ideas to life in the digital realm. Off the screen, I'm often found lifting weights, competing in pickup volleyball or basketball games, or engrossed in the latest biotech discoveries.

This website is a window into my journey, a place where I hope to share insights, projects, and discoveries with those who have stumbled upon it. Whether you're here to explore my programming endeavors, engage in discussions about biotechnology, or simply connect, welcome aboard!
